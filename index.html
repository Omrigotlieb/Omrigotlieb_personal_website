TangiPlan

TangiPlan is a prototype of a tangible assistive technology intended to improve executive functioning during morning routines among children and youth with ADHD. It comprised out of six tangible objects, representing tasks that need to be completed during a child’s morning routine, and a tablet application used for planning tasks and pairing them with objects.
